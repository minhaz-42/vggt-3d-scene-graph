\caption{The pipeline. Sparse RGB views feed two \emph{frozen} foundation models---VGGT for
world-point geometry and OWLv2 for open-vocabulary labeled boxes. Detections are lifted to 3D,
fused across views into object nodes, and annotated with spatial relations. Our one addition is a
post-fusion \emph{duplicate-aware merge} (amber): same-label nodes whose 3D boxes overlap are
collapsed into a single object, correcting the over-counting that cross-view fusion leaves behind.}
