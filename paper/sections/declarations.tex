\section*{Data Availability}

The RGB-D source sequences used in this study are publicly available from the TUM
RGB-D benchmark. The selected sparse-view manifests, generated scene-graph summaries,
annotation review packets, checked annotation JSON files, and metric tables are
provided as supplementary research artifacts. Model checkpoints and third-party
components follow their respective licenses.
